About Michael

He built a security framework now used by Fortune 500 companies. He founded ten ventures across sectors that have no obvious throughline — until you meet him.

Michael de Geus operates at the intersection of tactical intelligence, entrepreneurial strategy, and operational execution. His career has taken him from special operations environments to the boardrooms of multinational corporations — not as a consultant who observes, but as an operator who builds.

The security platform he developed, Shadow, distilled years of field experience into frameworks that Fortune 500 security teams now use to assess, train, and respond. His book, The Corporate Security Playbook, was written while he was actively running the operations it describes.

The ten ventures span tactical gear, performance beverages, apparel technology, marine operations, and beyond. The industries differ. The founding philosophy is identical: find the elegant solution hiding inside a problem everyone else has given up on.
